## A Step Toward Privacy

### When an apartment no longer holds the whole day

At some point, the question becomes wider than the number of bedrooms.

It becomes about where the work call ends, where the family evening begins, where guests stay downstairs, and where the private zone remains closed.

A standard apartment often puts every scenario on one level: living room, bedrooms, kitchen, children, work, rest.

A duplex format solves this through simple architecture: one level for movement, one level for privacy.

### A morning where everyone keeps their own rhythm

Someone has already gone downstairs for coffee.
Someone is still sleeping upstairs.
Children are getting ready in their own zone.
Parents start the day with calmer movement and fewer unnecessary crossings.

This type of home works better when people inside the family have different schedules, habits and speeds of life.

The main value here is separation.

### A day where the lower level carries the movement

The active part of the home stays downstairs: living room, kitchen, hosting space, work, daily routines and access to the terrace.

This is the level that can stay open.
For people, conversations, calls, food, guests and movement.

The upper level keeps another function: bedrooms, quiet, private terraces and a pause after the day.

This format is closer to the logic of a small private home, while keeping apartment ownership, building security and access to shared amenities.

### An evening where the home separates people without distance

For a couple, it gives more breathing space within one address.

For a family, it creates a clear structure: children and bedrooms upstairs, shared life and living space downstairs.

For a single owner, it gives extra room for work, rest, hosting and a personal rhythm.

For a family with elderly parents, it allows people to live together while keeping separate zones and reducing daily friction.

The layout matters more than the marketing name.
It defines how people meet, move and separate every day.

### Weekends close to home

Arjan suits a calmer residential scenario in Dubai.

Miracle Garden, Butterfly Garden, My City Centre Al Barsha, Global Village and Mall of the Emirates are all nearby.

This gives simple leisure options close to home: a family walk, a shopping trip, seasonal events, an evening outside, or quick daily errands.

The scenario fits buyers who want a practical city radius without the constant pressure of central districts.

### Why buyers choose this format

This type of penthouse is chosen when someone needs personal territory inside the city.

The value is in the working structure of daily life:

— two levels;
— terraces;
— separation between guests and bedrooms;
— a private upper zone;
— less of the standard apartment feeling;
— building security, parking and amenities;
— a more accessible entry ticket compared with penthouses in central Dubai districts.

This is a scenario between an apartment and a villa.

Villa logic — without the full villa load.
